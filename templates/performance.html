Design Notes

1
Deck Tabs — Full Width

Two tabs span the full screen width. The active deck (Deck A = LIVE, Deck B = CUE) shows a live dot indicator. Switching tabs swaps the waveform, timeline, and mode selection — everything scoped to that deck.

1b
Per-Deck Timeline

Each deck shows: waveform, scrub bar with elapsed/remaining time, and the color-map strip with the current section highlighted. Deck B loads cued at 0:00 with its full saved color map visible so the DJ can preview what's coming.

2
Per-Deck Mode Selection

Mode (Chill / Bounce / Hype / Drop) is selected per deck and sits directly under the timeline. When you switch to Deck B you see its mode state independently — so the DJ can pre-set the incoming track's mood before the transition.

3
Effects Panel — Always Visible

The FX panel lives below the deck tabs and is always accessible regardless of which deck is active. Intensity knob (270° arc, currently at 70%) controls the global FX output level. FX pads trigger effects: tap = instant, hold = sustained.

4
Intensity Knob

Hardware-style rotary knob. Arc track shows the 270° range (7 o'clock → 5 o'clock). Filled arc + tick indicator show current position. Planned: rotate gesture in the iOS app. Value label shows 0–100%.

5
Zone Mini-Controls

Per-zone energy sliders always visible at the bottom. No navigation needed to tweak zone levels during a set.
