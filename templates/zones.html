Design Notes

1
Zone Cards

One card per zone. Collapsed by default in live performance — expanded here for setup. Toggle switch kills all fixtures in a zone without deleting it.

2
Color Palette

Dots represent colors this zone will cycle through. Selected dot (white ring) is active. Tap + to open color picker. Palette drives the Color Engine on the performance screen.

3
Energy Slider

Controls overall intensity — flash rate, brightness, movement speed. Not a dimmer. 0% = static ambient. 100% = maximum reactive behavior.

4
Per-Zone Mode

Each zone runs its own mode independently. Dance Floor can be Hype while Chill Area stays in Chill — no global override needed.

5
Zone Toggle (off state)

Chill Area is toggled off — zone card goes muted. Useful mid-set when an area closes. Re-enable with one tap.
