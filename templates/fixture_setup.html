Design Notes

1
Auto-Discovery

Scans the local Wi-Fi subnet on open. No IP entry, no manual pairing. Works with VOLMAX-compatible fixtures that broadcast via mDNS/Bonjour.

2
Zone Assignment

Tap any fixture row to pick a zone. No channel numbers — just "Dance Floor", "Booth", "Chill Area". Fixtures inherit zone color + energy settings.

3
New Fixture Detection

Green highlight + NEW badge for fixtures seen for the first time. Prompts assignment before leaving setup.

4
Offline Fixtures

Greyed dot + "last seen" timestamp. Retains zone assignment so re-pairing is instant next session.

5
Rename (tap & hold)

Long-press a fixture to rename it. Stored locally. Replaces the factory mDNS hostname with a human-readable label.
