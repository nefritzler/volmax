Design Notes

1
Zone Cards — 2×2 Grid

Four zones displayed in a 2×2 grid. Zone names are editable inline and saved to localStorage. Each card maintains an independent fixture list.

2
Fixture Discovery Modal

Tap "+" on any zone card to open a discovery panel scoped to that zone. Scan returns dummy fixtures — tap "Add" to assign to the zone. The fixture is removed from the discovery list when added.

3
Test Button

Flashes all fixtures in the zone white for 1 second to help physically identify which fixtures belong to a zone. Stub for now — console.logs the zone index.

4
Default Color + Brightness

Each zone has a default idle color (color picker) and brightness (slider). These are used when the zone is not actively reading a live signal. Saved to localStorage.
